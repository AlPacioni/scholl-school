Glossary

Dock Basin
Enclosed water area for loading, unloading, and vessel service.
Shipyard
Industrial site where ships are built, repaired, or converted.
Intermodal Logistics
Freight movement across ship, rail, and road networks.
Tonnage
Measurement scale for cargo volume or vessel capacity.
Transshipment
Transfer of cargo between transport modes or vessels.
Convoy Support
Port and repair support for grouped wartime shipping operations.
