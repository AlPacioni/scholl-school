Epoche 2

Während des Zweiten Weltkrieges (1939-1945)

Industrieumstellung, Luftangriffe, Arbeitsbelastung und strategische Militaernutzung veraenderten das gesamte Hafen-Okosystem.

Industrial and Social Effects

  • Shipyard output was redirected toward war-priority maritime tasks.1
  • Urban districts and infrastructure faced heavy bombing in 1944-1945.2
  • Civilian housing and labor stability were severely disrupted.
